    Mental Health

    Our story focuses on mental health-specfically depression. Lots of people always refer to this term whenever they are sad, and do not actually take the literal meaning of the word into accound. In class during our week 7 discussion, we talked about a video by Ian Bogust. It's kind of like when Bogust opens up his presentation during UX week in 2013 by discussing the topics of games, and how they should be "fun". However, he goes on to say that the word used to describe pleasure and enjoyment is actually the exact opposite and we have coined this term to a different meaning. When people say they are deppresed or have deppression when they are sad, it reminds me of how they coined the term as a form of being sad in society rather than the literal meaning.

    It was important to make sure that people know what deppression actual is and how it feels like, which is why we decided to focus on this specific mental illness. In our story, we cover the illness by diving into Bowies conscience and showing how she feels when facing decisions. We wanted the overall vibes given off of Bowie to show the user what she is going through so we also tried to use pictures that visualize it well.

    Our Project

    Our story follows Bowie, a girl suffering from severe deppresion and anxiety. Her grandma has recently passed away making things much worse for her. Her parents argue a lot and she feels isolated. She is thrown a curveball and sets off on a journey to self discovery. Hopefully our story gives an insight to the effects deppression can have on a persons mental health.
